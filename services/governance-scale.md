# Service: Governance & Scale

**Phase 3 of the Fairness Maturity Model**

---

## Overview

Enterprise-wide fairness programs with governance frameworks, executive communication, and organizational change management. This phase transforms individual interventions into sustained organizational capability.

---

## What's Included

### 1. Integration Framework
- Connect fairness components across teams
- Workflow design for continuous fairness
- Information flow mapping
- Dependency management

### 2. Organizational Rollout
- Multi-team coordination strategy
- RACI matrix for fairness responsibilities
- Training and enablement programs
- Change management support

### 3. Governance Structure
- AI Ethics Committee charter
- Fairness gates in CI/CD pipelines
- Escalation procedures
- Audit trail requirements

### 4. Executive Communication
- CEO/Board presentation
- ROI analysis and business case
- Risk assessment framework
- Success metrics and KPIs

### 5. Regulatory Alignment
- EU AI Act compliance mapping
- Documentation requirements
- Audit preparation
- Ongoing compliance monitoring
